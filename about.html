Welcome to the Movie Ratings Site

This site is a simple way to explore and rate some of the most popular movies ever made. Our movie list is preloaded with hundreds of well‑known titles, so you can browse through them and share your opinion by giving each film a rating from 1 to 100.

To get started, use the navigation links above to move between pages. - On the Home page, you’ll find an overview of the site. - The Movies page displays the full list of films. - The Rate a Movie page lets you select a title and submit your rating. - The Ratings and Top Rated pages show how movies stack up.

Whether you’re revisiting classics or checking out fan favorites, this site makes it easy to keep track of ratings and discover which films rise to the top.
